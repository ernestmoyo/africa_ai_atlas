## Primary Data Sources Used

### 1. World Bank Open Data API
- **URL:** https://data.worldbank.org
- **Indicators pulled:**
  - `IT.NET.USER.ZS` — Internet users (% of population), 2023
  - `EG.ELC.ACCS.ZS` — Access to electricity (% of population), 2023
  - `IT.NET.BBND.P2` — Fixed broadband subscriptions (per 100 people), 2023
  - `SE.TER.ENRR` — Tertiary enrollment rate (%), most recent year
- **Coverage:** All 54 African countries
- **Data year:** 2022–2024 (most recent available)
- **Access method:** REST API (JSON format)

### 2. Oxford Insights Government AI Readiness Index 2025
- **URL:** https://oxfordinsights.com/ai-readiness/government-ai-readiness-index-2025/
- **Data:** Country scores across 6 pillars (Policy Capacity, AI Infrastructure, Governance, Public Sector Adoption, Development & Diffusion, Resilience)
- **Coverage:** 195 countries (54 African)
- **Used for:** Benchmark comparison and validation of our scoring

### 3. Carnegie Endowment for International Peace
- **URL:** https://carnegieendowment.org/posts/2025/09/understanding-africas-ai-governance-landscape
- **Data:** Complete list of 12 African countries with published national AI strategies
- **Coverage:** Continental AI governance landscape analysis

### 4. UNESCO AI Strategy Observatory
- **URL:** https://unesco.org
- **Data:** AI strategy development status, ethics frameworks
- **Coverage:** 29 African countries engaged in AI policy work

### 5. UNCTAD / Data Protection Africa
- **URL:** https://unctad.org/page/data-protection-and-privacy-legislation-worldwide
- **URL:** https://dataprotection.africa
- **Data:** Data protection law status per country
- **Coverage:** All 54 African countries

### 6. Partech Africa Tech VC Reports (2024, 2025)
- **URL:** https://partechpartners.com/africa-reports/
- **Data:** VC funding by country, deal counts, startup ecosystem metrics
- **Coverage:** Continental with country breakdowns

### 7. AfriLabs Network
- **URL:** https://afrilabs.com
- **Data:** 514 innovation hubs across 53 African countries
- **Coverage:** Continental tech hub mapping

### 8. Africa Data Center Trackers
- **Sources:** Systalink, African Exponent, Mordor Intelligence
- **Data:** Data center counts by country, hyperscaler presence
- **Coverage:** All African countries with data center infrastructure

### 9. Disrupt Africa Funding Reports
- **URL:** https://disruptafrica.com
- **Data:** Startup funding, deal counts by country
- **Coverage:** Continental

### 10. Global Center for AI Research
- **URL:** https://globalcenter.ai
- **Data:** AI strategy analysis, policy mapping
- **Coverage:** African countries with AI strategies

## Data Limitations & Notes

1. **World Bank data lag:** Most recent data is 2023; some countries have 2022 as latest
2. **Internet penetration anomaly:** Kenya shows 35% (World Bank) vs higher estimates from other sources — World Bank methodology differs from ITU
3. **Small island states:** Cabo Verde, Comoros, São Tomé — limited innovation ecosystem data
4. **Conflict zones:** Somalia, South Sudan, Sudan, Libya — data may be unreliable or outdated
5. **Francophone gap:** French-language AI activity may be undercounted in English-language sources
6. **Startup data:** Concentrated in Big 4 (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Egypt) — smaller ecosystems likely undercounted
