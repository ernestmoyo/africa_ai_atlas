## Scoring Guide

- **AI Use Cases**: Estimated count of documented, active AI use cases (Low: 1-5, Medium: 6-15, High: 16-30, Very High: 30+)
- **Public Sector AI**: None / Nascent / Developing / Established / Advanced
- **Financial AI**: None / Nascent / Developing / Established / Advanced
- **Agriculture AI**: None / Nascent / Developing / Established / Advanced
- **Health AI**: None / Nascent / Developing / Established / Advanced
- **Sector Diversity**: Number of sectors with active AI deployments (out of ~8 key sectors: finance, health, agriculture, education, energy, transport, government, security)

### Countries Where Deployment Was UNDERESTIMATED (Flags)

1. **Ghana (Rank 9, Composite 46.8)** — UNDERESTIMATED. The $1B Ghana-UAE AI Hub deal (signed May 2025, construction starting 2026) is a game-changer. EGDI score of 0.6317 is very strong (highest in West Africa). **Recommend increasing P5 from 38 to 45 and reclassifying Health AI from "Developing" to "Established."**

2. **Cameroon (Rank 19, Composite 26.8)** — UNDERESTIMATED. Cameroon launched a comprehensive National AI Strategy (SNIA) with 2040 roadmap, 7 strategic pillars, plan for sovereign LLM ("GPT Cameroon"), and 15 edge computing nodes. Active AI deployment in wildlife anti-poaching and health sector predictive outbreak models. **Recommend increasing P1 from 35 to 42 and P5 from 18 to 22.**

3. **Burkina Faso (Rank 35, Composite 14.2)** — UNDERESTIMATED. Despite political instability, Burkina Faso launched national AI action plan workshop (Aug 2025), Digital Development Observatory (Oct 2025), $5.4M digital supervision center, and a public AI education campaign. AI-language partnership with national broadcaster. This is more AI activity than the "Very Low (1-2)" rating implies. **Recommend increasing AI Use Cases to Low (3-5) and P1 from 22 to 28.**

4. **Togo (Rank 24, Composite 22.0)** — UNDERESTIMATED. The Novissi program is being scaled into a comprehensive national social protection system (ASTRE) reaching 1.24M people by 2029. 5. **Zimbabwe (Rank 22, Composite 25.4)** — UNDERESTIMATED. National AI Strategy (2026-2030) approved by Cabinet Oct 2025. Dawa Health deploying multimodal AI for cervical cancer (96.7% accuracy) and anemia detection. UNDP AI Innovation Week with 270+ applicants for MineTech Challenge. Cassava Technologies (headquartered by Zimbabwean founder Masiyiwa) provides continental AI infrastructure. **Recommend increasing P5 from 18 to 22 and Health AI from "Nascent" to "Developing."**

AI Center of Excellence at University of Zambia (partnership with Google, Sept 2024). eShandi AI-powered challenger bank expanding to 3 countries. AgriPredict AI crop disease diagnosis app. UNDP solar-hybrid AI-enabled public services. **Recommend increasing P5 from 18 to 22 and Financial AI from "Nascent" to "Developing."**

7. **Benin (Rank 15, Composite 28.4)** — UNDERESTIMATED. GPT.BJ chatbot deployed for legal information. JaimeMaLangue project for local language AI. National data center with ML-as-a-Service. First voice-to-voice AI model in Fon language. EGDI of 0.42+ and strong Oxford Insights scores. **Recommend increasing P5 from 22 to 28.**

8. **Mozambique (Rank 44, Composite 10.6)** — UNDERESTIMATED. AI-powered early warning system for climate disasters with UNDP/World Bank (active deployment using ML + satellite + data analytics). This is a specific, documented AI deployment. **Recommend increasing P5 from 8 to 10.**
