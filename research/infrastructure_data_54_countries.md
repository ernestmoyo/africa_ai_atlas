## Methodology Notes

1. **Internet Penetration** figures are primarily from ITU 2023 data as compiled by World Population Review (accessed March 2026). Morocco's 2025 figure (92%) from Statista/DataReportal is also referenced.

2. **Mobile Broadband Subscriptions** per 100 people are approximated using the ITU 5-tier classification for Africa published in ITU Facts & Figures 2024. Exact country-level values are available from ITU DataHub but require interactive query. Approximate tier values are used where exact data was unavailable.

3. **Fixed Broadband Subscriptions** per 100 people are from World Bank (IT.NET.BBND.P2) and TradingEconomics for 2023. For countries without specific data, estimates are based on ITU tier classification.

4. **Electricity Access** figures are from the World Bank SDG7 tracking dataset (EG.ELC.ACCS.ZS), 2022 data via World Population Review.

5. **Cloud Infrastructure** classification is based on DataCenterMap.com (249 facilities across 40 countries in Africa) and Business Elites Africa Top 10 ranking (2025):
   - "Multiple" = 10+ data centers with major cloud providers present
   - "Limited" = 1-9 data centers
   - "None" = No known colocation/commercial data centers
