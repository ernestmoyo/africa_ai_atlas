# Africa AI Atlas - Innovation & Ecosystem Data (All 54 Countries)

**Compiled: March 2026 | Data period: 2024-2025**

## Methodology & Source Notes

Data compiled from: Partech Africa Tech VC Report 2025, Disrupt Africa Funding Report 2025, Briter Bridges/Africa Investment Report 2025, StartupList Africa, Tracxn, AfriLabs (514 hubs across 53 countries), GSMA Ecosystem Accelerator, WIPO World Intellectual Property Indicators 2025, Deep Learning Indaba/IndabaX network, Zindi.africa (100K+ users across 52 countries), Crunchbase, TechCabal, and national government AI strategy documents.

**Key:** Where exact figures are unavailable for a specific country, estimates are marked with `~`. "Minimal" or "N/A" indicates no verifiable data was found. AI-specific VC figures are separated from total tech VC where possible. Patent data reflects total patent filings (AI-specific patents are not separately reported for most African countries by WIPO).
